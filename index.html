Syed Shujaat Ali

Researcher & Global Economics Graduate

I am a researcher and a Summa Cum Laude graduate in Global Economics from the University of Central Asia (UCA), where I was honored as the Valedictorian of the Class of 2026. My academic and research focus is centered on quantitative macroeconomics, deep-dive econometrics, and applied economics.

Throughout my undergraduate journey, I served extensively as a Teaching Assistant for core courses including Econometrics, Microeconomics, Macroeconomics, and Discrete Mathematics, and led both the Economics Club and the Organization for Educational Change (OEC) as President. Moving forward, my work builds on structural research foundational pipelines, including my time spent evaluating global financial cycle spillovers within the Monetary Policy Department at the State Bank of Pakistan.
